FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior yoga experience?+

No prior experience is required for our 100-hour and 200-hour programs. We welcome complete beginners as well as experienced practitioners seeking certification.

Is the certification recognized internationally?+

Yes, our 200-hour and 300-hour programs are registered with Yoga Alliance USA, making your certification valid and recognized in over 100 countries worldwide.

What is included in the course fees?+

Fees include accommodation, three vegetarian meals daily, all training sessions, course materials, excursions, and your Yoga Alliance certification upon completion.

When is the best time to visit Rishikesh?+

The best months are September to November and February to May, offering pleasant weather ideal for outdoor practice and exploration of the surrounding region.

How do I get to Rishikesh?+

The nearest airport is Jolly Grant in Dehradun, 35 km away. Rishikesh is also well-connected by train to Haridwar and by road from Delhi via regular bus services.
